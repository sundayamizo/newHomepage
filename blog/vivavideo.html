VivaVideo Android Video Editor
29/09/2014 3:22 PM, Bilkhawthlir

Android OS hmangtu te tan thil kan share leh teh ang! Then khat tan thil thar a nih laiin thenkhat tan hriat hnu a ni bawk ang!



VivaVideo , Android Video editor changkang leh hmantlak zet mai hi han hmel hriat ve teh! Google Play Store ah emaw, VivaVideo .apk file google ah zawng la, download tur i hmu teuh ang!
Enge he application hian a tih theih i lo en ang aw;
  • Thlalak Slide Show siam nan a duhthusam tling ve tak a ni!
  • I thlalak dahkhawm (slide show atan) te chu a lan rei zawng pakhat te te in i adjust thei!
  • Chung thlalak karah chuan Video clip hran i zep tep thei!
  • Theme hran hran thlan tur leh download belh tur a awm; heng theme te hi changkang hlawm tak an ni!
  • I music duh zawng zawng i zep tep thei bawk!
  • I aw i dah thei (dubbed) thei bawk
  • Sub-title/ thu i chhu tel thei bawk!
Heng bakah hian sawi hmaih ka la nei nual awm e! A bak erawh chu nangmah ngeiin han belchiang ve teh le! I Android phone kha a tih theih tam tak tih tir lo va dah trawl mai mai lo khan , a nihna ang tak kha han hmang chhuak ve teh le!
A Pro version a a free in a awm a, i zawn hmuh chuan a version free ai chuan a tha zawk fe a nia.
Thil dang pawh ka la rawn share zel dawn nia!
