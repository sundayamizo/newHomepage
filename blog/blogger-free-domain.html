BLOGGER TAN FREE DOMAIN (.TK, .ML, .GA, .CF, .GQ) 2016
25/01/2016 12:01 AM, Ramhlun South, Aizawl

Blogger  hmang thin i nih chuan kan sawi tum hi enge a awmzia i hre nel nual turah ngai ila!
I blog siam kha URL a nei a, entirnan http://sundayakrypton.blogspot.com tih te a  ni thin. A thui deuh teh duah a ni tiraw?



blogspot.com / blogspot.in (subdomain) tih lai hi .com / .in /.net /.org (domain an ti a) tih in i thlak thei a. Entirnan:
www.sundayakrypton.blogspot.com tih duah ai chuan www.sundayakrypton.com tih mai khan a fel fai in a changkang hmel zawk bawk te i han ti teh ang!

Amarawhchu, heng .com, .in, .net,.org tih ang chi ho hi chu man a lei ngai an ni! Domain provider tam tak a awm a; heng GoDaddy   , BigRock  leh a dang tam tak ah te hian engtik lai pawn a lei theih reng a ni. A rate chung chang ah chuan in a in an loh bakah a man hi a dang (fluctuate)  ve reng a ni.

I blog domain tur a i pawisai seng ui si a nih chuan domain changkang lutuk lo (!) , mahse a thlawn a neih mai theih a awm a, kan lo hrilh ru ve ang che!

A hnuaia step ang hian ti zel la, i hriatthiam loh chuan comment lamah min zawt mai dawn nia.

STEP 1: Freenom  ah hian lut la, in Register rawh le!

STEP 2: Registration process hrang hrang i tih zawhah Register New Domain tih ah khan lut rawh!

     
STEP 3: A hnuaia mi lo lang ang ah khan i free domain duh chu type lut rawh le! Domain hi .ml , .tk, .cf, .gq leh .ga a awm a, khawi emaw ber hi i hmang thei a ni.



STEP 4: I domain duh chu a la awm a nih chuan a hnuai a mi ang khuan , Checkout ah khan i lut dawn nia!


STEP 5: Forward this domain ah kal la, i blog URL kha type la, 12 Months @ Free kha thlang la, Continue rawh.



STEP 6: A step dawt leh ah chuan i Details Fill Up na tur a lo awm ang. Kha kha pangngai takin Fill Up la, and Terms and Condition kha Accept thlap la, i Order i complete dawn nia.

STEP 7: I tih zawh fel hun ah Domains > My Domains ah lut la, i domain order te chu a lo awm ang! I domain siam zawh chiah ah khan Manage Domain ah lut la, a hnuaia Pic ang khuan a lo awm ang. Management Tools >> Nameservers ah khan i lut dawn nia.


STEP 8: Use Default Nameserver select la, Change Nameserver click leh ang!



 STEP 9:  I blogger account ah log in la, Settings ah khan Publishing > Blog address ah khan
"+ Setup a 3rd party URL for your blog " tih kha i hmu ang tah khan click la, i domain siam fel sa kha i type ang a, a hnuai a mi ang khuan a lo lang ang. Arrow mark sen a ka chhinchhiah te khu i chhinchhiah ang.



 STEP 10: Freenom ah khan lut leh la, Manage Freenom DNS ah khan lut la a hnuai a pic ang khuan i fill up dawn nia.


 STEP 11: Save la,Blogger Settings ah let leh ta a dik thlap tawh ang!


A dik lo emaw harsatna i nei a nih chuan comments ah emaw email sundayakent@gmail.com ah min mail mai dawn nia!
