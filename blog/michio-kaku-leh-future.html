Pu Michio Kaku Leh Future Hrilhlawk!
09/01/2014 12:09 AM, Bilkhawthlir

He Japanese-American Physicist ropui lutuk mai hian risk a la huaiin ,a confi em em mai a. Prediction a siam huai thin khawp mai.' Kum 2020 a rawn thlen meuh chuan 'computer' tih thumal hi English atangin a bo tawh ang .... 'a ti mauh mai.



'Thla 18 dan vel zelah kan technology te hi a let thawk thawk in a changkan belh zel a, a tlawm zel bawk a. Chuvangin kum 8 hnu velah chuan chip te pawh cheng tlem man chauh a la ni thuai ang. Kan Computer a chip awm kha duh tawk mai lovin kan thil tin rengah kan dah tawh ang;kan furniture, car , thawmhnaw leh bungbel te thlengin ....chip kan dah vek tawh ang . 'Chip' tih thumal a lo lar dawn tawh em avangin 'computer tih thumal chu English language atang hian a bo deuh thaw tawh ang' a ti a ni.

He City College Of New York Theoretical Physicist ,Kum 67 mi hian prediction siam hi a nuam a ti ve hrim hlim a, A lehkhabu pakhat 'Physics Of The Future' ah chuan kum 2020 ah chuan driver ngai lo car te, Kum 2030 ah taksa peng siam chawp (artificial organ) te a awm tawh tur thu a sawi bawk.
Dr.Kaku vek hian ,' Thumal pakhat bo leh mai thei tur pakhat chu 'tumor tih hi a ni. Kan toilet ah te emaw chip kan dah ang a, chu chuan kan zun leh ek vel a lo exam ang a, cancer emaw natna hlauhawm a awm dawn tih a lo hriat chuan a sawi ang a, tumor a insiam hma( a pawithui hma) in kan hre vek mai dawn a ni. Tichuan kan bang a screen ah chuan Doctor thiam tak , Robodoc an tih chu a lo lang ang a, a ni chuan 99% vel a dik inkawlna a lo pe ang che ang a, tumor a insiam hman ngai reng reng dawn lo a ni.' a ti bawk.
